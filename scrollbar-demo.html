Custom Scrollbar Demo

This is a standalone demo of the custom scrollbar for the Lemon Tattoo website. Scroll down to see it in action.

About Lemon Tattoo

Lemon Tattoo is a modern tattoo studio specializing in unique, artistic designs. Our custom scrollbar features a thin, glass-like translucent track with a lemon-inspired gradient thumb that glows on hover.

Keep scrolling to experience the smooth, lightweight scrolling experience. This design is fully responsive and works across devices.
